About Us

Fresh Ingredients

We make most of our frozen yogurt with natural, fresh ingredients, and provide an amazing selection of toppings! Many flavors of our yogurt are made in-house each morning.

Unique Flavors

In addition, we have an extensive selection of unique house flavors! Go to flavors and each image with the banner are those we hand craft ourselves! Some very unique flavors include Sweet Azuki, Thai Tea, and Matcha Green Tea. We also provide an amazing selection of toppings, which can be viewed here.

Ecofriendly

We also compost any ingredients that are unused. Items such as strawberry leaves, coffee grinds, etc. are sent to the garden in Revelle College to be reused for the plants growing there!

Healthy

We seek to meet all dietary needs. We have various options for those with certain dietary restrictions. Some options include vegan, lactose free, sugar free, and dairy free. You can view the dietary information of each flavor on the Flavors Page.
